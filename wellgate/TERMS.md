- Subscriptions are processed by **Stripe**. By subscribing, you also agree to Stripe’s terms where applicable.
- **Manage your subscription** (switch plan, update payment method, cancel) through the **Stripe Customer Portal**, available from WellGate settings after you subscribe.
- **Switching plans:** You may switch between Explorer and BlackBox in the Customer Portal. If you are on a free trial and change plan or quantity, the trial **ends immediately** and Stripe **starts charging** for the selected plan without waiting for the trial to finish.
- **Proration:** When you change plan or quantity, Stripe issues a **credit** for the unused portion of your current billing period and **charges** the new price (or adjusted quantity) for the remainder of the period. Proration is **invoiced immediately** at the time of the change.
- **Downgrades:** If you switch to a lower-priced plan (e.g. BlackBox → Explorer), the change takes effect **immediately** under the proration rules above.
- **Cancellations:** You may cancel your subscription in the Customer Portal. Cancellation is scheduled for the **end of the current billing period** — you keep access until that date unless otherwise stated at cancel time. Stripe may **ask for a cancellation reason**; that reason is processed by Stripe as described in our Privacy Policy.
- **Refund requests:** Contact wellgate.support@lumeniad.com within **14 days** of a charge if you believe a refund is warranted. Statutory consumer rights in Denmark and your country of residence apply where mandatory.
